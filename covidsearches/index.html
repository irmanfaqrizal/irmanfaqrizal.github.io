Analysis of Google Searches on "Covid-19"

In this article we will do an analysis of Google Searches on "Covid-19". We will show several statistics taken from an application from google called "Google Trends". Then we will discuss each of them and try to relate with the real world events.

We will first try to understand the graph shown above. This graph is about comparision of google searches on the topic of coronavirus (Covid-19) compared to other common topics such as Weather, Music, and Sport. The graph is directly taken from google and it is interactive, readers may put their cursor on the graph and the details will show up. It is important to understand that google uses popularity scale from 0 to 100, with 100 being the peak of popularity (in this case it is March 12, 2020).
