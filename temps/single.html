Perfect Romance

  • Adventures
February 9, 2025

After purposelessly scrolling through countless of videos on Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok (cyclically, for god knows why), I felt some short of pain in my chest. It was a similar feeling that I normally experience when I am outside looking at happy couples due to loneliness and jealousy. I could not assess the cause of this feeling. However, I think I need to get my shit together asap.


I recently finished Metaphor: ReFantazio, and I like how the game simply refer to you as The Protagonist. Also, a minor spoiler, the guardian of this main character keeps reminding him about his purpose. I am a crappy writer so bare with me regarding the relations between sentences. But, basically, I think if I want to be The Protagonist of my own life I have to first figure out my purpose. I understand that they say such a realisation is particullarly common when you hit your 30s. However, in this post, I would like to elaborate what I have in my mind and how I feel about life objectives.
