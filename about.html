About the young man

Irman spent his childhood in a peaceful small city in Indonesia called Jombang. Growing up, he was silly and extremely naive. One time he decided to rebel against the exam system in the high school, by not studying for the exam. He barely graduated from high school, but he was lucky nevertheless because it could have been worse.


He went to a private university in the nearby city, and choose to learn computer science. This decision was taken without any significant thought whatsoever. He thinks that since he likes computer game then he might also like computer science.


Fast forward 5 years later, he is now doing his master's degree in Grenoble, France.
