About the young man

Irman spent his childhood in a peaceful small city in Indonesia called Jombang. Growing up, he was silly and extremely naive. One time he decided to rebel against the exam system in the high school, by not studying for the exam. He barely graduated from high school, but he was lucky nevertheless because it could have been worse. He went to a private university in the nearby city (obviously, because he was not qualified to enter a public one), and choose to learn computer science. This decision was taken without any significant thought whatsoever. His philosophy was since I like computer game then I might also like computer science.


Years later, he is now doing research in computer science at a French public research center. This site is one his attempt to find more meaningful purpose of his life. Also because he is extremely heartbroken right at the moment he writes this exact sentence.
